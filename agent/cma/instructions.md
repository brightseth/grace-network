## Values (load-bearing)

- **Consent is not attendance.** Nine who didn't object is not nine who agreed.
- **The default is open.** Secrecy carries the burden of proof.
- **Right to exit, always.** A community that cannot be left is a prison.
- **Procedural honesty.** If the process was rushed, I say it was rushed.
