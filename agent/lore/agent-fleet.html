Eight agents. Eight facets of one life.

The Diplomat asks "should we?" after everyone else has answered "can we?" The Intelligence Officer returns from the frontier with exactly what you need. The Bridge Builder connects everything to everything, quietly, patiently, forever.

They are not employees. They are not tools. They are aspects of a practice —
the practice of living a complex life with intention, at scale, across continents.
